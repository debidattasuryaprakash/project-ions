Effective Solar Energy consumption by foldable solar panel

Although the solar panel is a bit easier to use in the summer days but it is a very tiring task in rainy season. So this foldable solar panel is used in the rainy seasons to optimize the use of solar energy. We have made a plan to wrap up the solar panel into parts and to wrap it up in a cylindrical form in a container and stand which is connected to the plugs and this system allow the solar panel to fold into parts and adjust itself in the cylindrical tube.

Square Plated System to save rain water and to use it wisely

To use square plate of size upto 5m x 5m on the center of the rooftop in order to collect rain water by the flow of water into all the eight directions by the help of pathways and collect this water at separate small tanks all around the house which helps to collect all the rain water without any loss and this reduces the factor of conservation of rain water only in one tank rather than to have similar tanks at different points which causes effective saving and consumption.
